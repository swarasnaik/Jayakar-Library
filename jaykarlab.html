About Jayakar Library

The Jayakar Library at Savitribai Phule Pune University is a premier academic library with a rich collection of books, journals, theses, and e-resources. It serves as a vital hub for students, teachers, and researchers across disciplines. The library is known for its advanced digital facilities, reading rooms, research support, and vast archives of academic resources.
